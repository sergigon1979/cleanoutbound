<p>
<strong>Is outbound sales eating itself alive?</strong>
</p>

<p>
The practices everyone treats as standard still work for the teams who rely on them, but they are sliding into diminishing returns while poisoning the well for everyone else. I’ve spent years helping teams drive growth, watching the wins pile up locally while the ecosystem absorbed the costs. That conflict defines this project. This is my attempt to understand how we got here, and whether a clean exit is possible.
</p>

<p>
This is written for those growing skeptical of the received wisdom. If you still believe the standard playbook has a future, you may find this an uncomfortable read.
</p>
